RecipeBuddy

I created RecipeBuddy as part of a 2-week Term Project for my Freshman Fall Fundamentals of Programming Course. Utilizing Python and BeautifulSoup, I crafted this program in an attempt to offer solutions to food waste, and aid short-on-time individuals in the culinary journey.

RecipeBuddy serves as an all-in-one cookbook. It web scrapes and displays recipes based on the ingredients consumers have on hand and cuisine preference, sorting the recipes from least to greatest number of extra ingredients needed, time needed to prepare the recipe, or by the rating from its site. Users are also able to favorite recipes they enjoy, and view all of their favorited recipes in one page. Users are able to create their own recipes and view all of such recipes as well. Lastly, RecipeBuddy can generate recipePlans based on cuisine preferences, ingredients, and nutritional goals — a function ideal for those who aim to plan for the week ahead.
